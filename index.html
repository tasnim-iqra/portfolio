About Me

My name is Tasnim Jahan. I am a passionate photographer with a love for capturing moments that tell a story. My journey in photography started as a hobby and has grown into a deep-seated passion. I specialize in portrait and landscape photography, always seeking to find the beauty in the everyday. I am also a henna artist and I love to create beautiful designs.

This portfolio is a collection of my favorite work. I hope you enjoy it.
